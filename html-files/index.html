How to play:


Hi! Welcome to Bawls! The game is quite simple, the ball will bounce around and your goal is to not let it get passed you. You will earn points everytime you successfully keep the ball away.


To move, use the a and w keys. The a key will move the player left, while the w key will move the player right.


Simple!
